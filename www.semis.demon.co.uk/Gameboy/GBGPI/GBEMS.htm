GBEMS 


GameBoy Engine Management System
The GameBoy Engine Management System uses the Gameboy general purpose interface 'GBGPI' to convert a GBpocket into an engine management system with on screen display.  The purpose of the project was to develop a light weight and portable system to control a car for entering into the Shell ECO Marathon 2000.

Last year the car managed 2500 miles per gallon using a four stroke 25cc petrol engine with standard carburetor.  This year it is hoped that an improved engine and fuel injection system will push this even further.
