Term Project Functionality

  • This is a "soft" deadline of when you should have all functionality complete in your game
  • This deadline gives you two days to test your code for bugs and crashes, and make sure it is ready for the presentation at Mindscapes two days later
  • Due May 6, midnight
  • There is nothing to hand in or for me to check for this assignment. It is given to keep you on track for the presentation.

Purpose

At the time this assignment is due, we are two days away from our presentations of our games. By completing this assignment you will:

  • be on target with presenting your application on May 9.
  • have all of the functionality complete for your project

  • have debugged your code to work on a real GameBoy --- not just the emulator

  • have the opportunity to submit your completed code via blog or discussion list for valuable feedback from class members
