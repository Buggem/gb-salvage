Midterm

General

  • the midterm is 15% of your grade

  • the exam will be given in class on March 12, 2008

  • you cannot use anything that uses electricity or has an on/off switch for the exam. This means:
    • no computers
    • no calculators
  • you can bring in notes.

    • I highly recommend you print the GameBoy crib sheet and bring it to the exam

    • You should not need references outside of the crib sheet for the exam.
      • I will provide any other resources you need within the exam itself

exam format

The exam will be divided into sections:

  • write code which does such-and-such
  • hand-assemble some code. (I will give you relevant parts of the Z80 user's manual for the hand-assembly)
  • explain what is broken

  • GameBoy video

  • RGBDS macros
