2001-01-03 - !! VIRUS ALERT !! - Posted by: CyBuilder
DO NOT DOWNLOAD GAMES, APPLICATIONS, OR BOOT LOGOS FROM SOMEONE YOU DO NOT TRUST! THEY COULD BE INFECTED WITH VIRUSES THAT ERASE ALL OF THE FILES ON YOUR CYBIKO! No viruses have YET been reported but experiments have shown they are easy to produce.

Please note that this is the official web site for Tetris, Pacman, Asteroids, Flash Card Quiz, BootViewer, CyUCon, and Snake for Cybiko. The latest versions are always available here, first, and only those obtained from this server are guaranteed to be virus free.
