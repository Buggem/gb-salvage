Part #5800 is a dual-purpose PC board for adapting most 80-pin, or smaller, QFP or TSOP devices to 0.1 inch header spacing for easy breadboarding. One side supports 0.5mm spacing parts and the other side supports 0.8mm spacing parts. Soldering headers to the appropriate side determines which side to use. Because of the unique design of the board, any device 80 pins or less, can easily be supported. QFP parts will work regardless if each side has an odd or even number of pins. The board is 0.062 inches (1.57mm) thick and is made of FR4 laminate with plated-through holes. 60/40 tin-lead solder is electroplated over 1.5oz of copper material. The 4 large holes, in each corner, are just large enough to accept 2-56 (2mm) size screws. These can be obtained from Radio Shack (part #64-3010a) or similar places.

Here's just a very few examples of supported device package types:

PQFP/TQFP 32 (JEDEC MS-026 ABA, 0.8mm lead pitch, 7x7mm body)
PQFP/TQFP 44 (JEDEC MS-026 ACB, 0.8mm lead pitch, 10x10mm body)
PQFP/TQFP 64 (JEDEC MS-026 AEB, 0.8mm lead pitch, 14x14mm body)

TSOP 32 (JEDEC MO-142 BA, 0.5mm lead pitch, 8mm x 14mm body)
TSOP 40 (JEDEC MO-142 CA, 0.5mm lead pitch, 10mm x 14mm body)

TSOP 40 (JEDEC MO-142 CD, 0.5mm lead pitch, 10mm x 20mm body)
TSOP 48 (JEDEC MO-142 DD, 0.5mm lead pitch, 12mm x 20mm body)
TSOP 56 (JEDEC MO-142 EC, 0.5mm lead pitch, 14mm x 20mm body)

Note1: You need to supply your own 0.1" headers.

Note2: Not compatible with Meritec SKT-01-1098 56 pin TSOP socket.
